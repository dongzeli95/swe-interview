# SWE Senior / Staff Track

## Target

Senior (L5/E5/IC5) → Staff (L6/E6/IC6) SWE roles at product companies. Instacart, Meta, Google, Amazon, Uber, Stripe, DoorDash, etc.

## What this bar means (vs. mid-level)

- **Coding:** solve LC medium in 15 min, hard in 25–30 min, first cut, then discuss trade-offs. Bar isn't cleverness — it's clarity, correctness, complexity awareness, and communicating while you code.
- **System design:** two rounds are common at staff. Expect end-to-end scoping (functional/non-functional reqs, capacity math), decomposition into services, data model, API, storage choice, hot-path analysis, failure modes, and one or two deep-dives the interviewer picks.
- **Behavioral / leadership:** staff-level requires stories about ambiguity, cross-team influence, technical strategy, mentoring, and disagreeing well. Reuse the [Amazon Principal Leadership Prep](../../../Amazon%20Principal%20Leadership%20Prep.txt) doc as a source of hero stories.
